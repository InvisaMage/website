    • Known Problems

    Found a problem? Contact me.

    The navigation dropdown on mobile devices may shift over and clip content on small screens.


    Fixed

    The background color for the Terminal does not always apply.
    Blink based browsers stack the homepage cards incorrectly.
    Modals have no animations due to a bootstrap bug.
    I fixed this bug and then I liked the faster loading so now it's no longer a bug. It's a feature.


    Notes

    Tooltips are disabled on touchscreen devices.
    Modals and other ajaxed content may take awhile to load depending on your connection.
    Some content blocking extensions block the Hide Ads modal from loading.

    Ajaxed content does not work locally on most modern browsers such as Firefox and Chrome.
    A workaround for Firefox is to set this flag:
    security.fileuri.strict_origin_policy in about:config to false.
    This workaround is not ideal and should be reverted when done.
    A simple webserver serving the pages over localhost is more ideal.
