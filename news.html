    • Known Bugs / Problems

    Found a problem? Contact me.

    Scrolling back to the top of the page on a Mobile device can be laggy.
    Going to anchors clips content due to the fixed navigation bar.

    Fixed

    Currently the Water function does not work on Blink based browsers. Going to another page's anchor does not work correctly. (Stays at the top of the page).
    The Date Jump menu is only visible on the Projects tab while on a Mobile device.
    On the Floating Survival page, using the carousel will focus the page on it.
    The images on the iOS Web Application page are outdated.
    The Search function can act weird sometimes.
    Pages zoom out sometimes on smaller screens.
    The namePrompt function does not use a modal.
    Tables don't fit well on Mobile.
    The redirect to Google from declining the Terms or the Privacy policy.
    The news page has bigger font.
    Tooltips don't always load.



    Notes

    Tooltips are disabled on touchsreen devices.
    Modals and other ajaxed content may take awhile to load depending on your connection.
    Some content blocking extensions block the Hide Ads modal from loading.
    If you are viewing this site locally, cookies will not work on Blink based browsers.
    Cookies will not work locally as they are only set on secure connections.

    Ajaxed content does not work locally on Blink based browsers.
    A known workaround for this problem is to load the browser executable with a flag.
    Win + R > path/to/.exe -allow-file-access-from-files > OK
    Example: Win + R > C:\Program Files (x86)\Google\Chrome\Application\chrome.exe -allow-file-access-from-files > OK
