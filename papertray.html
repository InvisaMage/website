    • Description

    I found myself manually navigating to the PaperMC website to check for and download new builds.
    Any repetitive task should be automated so I made Papertray and some other utility scripts.
    Papertray can Backup, Trim, Update, and Start your PaperMC server.
    This was made public in the hopes it's useful to others.
    Feel free to use this as a template and modify to suit your needs.
